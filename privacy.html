Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Summary

FaceSwapOffline is an iPhone app for photo and video face swap editing. Face detection, replacement preparation, preview generation, and media processing are designed to run on your device. We do not operate servers that upload your photos, videos, face images, editing projects, or generated media for AI processing.

Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to FaceSwapOffline, the iPhone app and related hosted pages operated for the FaceSwapOffline product. In this policy, "we", "our", and "us" mean the developer and operator of FaceSwapOffline.

Information we do not collect

FaceSwapOffline does not provide a separate app account system or ask you to create a profile. We do not collect, store on our servers, sell, or share your original photos, videos, replacement face images, face templates, editing projects, or saved results.

We do not build biometric identity databases from your media, and we do not use your media or generated content to train AI models.

Consistent with our current App Store privacy disclosures and privacy manifest, the app currently does not include advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking SDKs, or developer-operated analytics services.

Local data on your device

To make editing faster and more reliable, the app may store imported media copies, temporary processing files, cached previews, face detection metadata, thumbnails, recent replacement faces, editing state, and saved media files locally on your device.

This local data remains on your device unless you choose to export, save, share, or back it up through iOS or another service you control. Deleting the app removes app-managed local data, but media already saved to Photos or shared outside the app is managed by you and by the services you use.

Permissions

  • Photos and videos: used so you can choose media to edit.
  • Add to Photos: used only when you choose to save exported photos or videos.
  • Local storage: used to import videos safely, cache previews, keep recent replacement faces, and create saved media files.

You can change Photos permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app.

Subscriptions and purchases

Subscriptions, renewals, cancellations, billing, and refunds are handled by Apple through StoreKit, your Apple ID, and the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card number, Apple ID password, or bank details.

The app may read StoreKit product information and your local verified purchase status so it can show purchase options, restore purchases, and determine which app features are available.

Network access and hosted pages

The app may connect to Apple services for StoreKit subscription products, purchases, entitlement checks, and App Store functionality. It may also open our hosted product page, Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, support links, or future static update information through secure web links.

Static hosting providers, such as GitHub Pages, may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, request time, and error logs according to their own infrastructure practices. We do not use those hosted pages to process your app media.

Children and minors

FaceSwapOffline is not intended for children under 13 or for anyone below the minimum age required by the laws in their location. Do not use the app to create, edit, export, or share media involving minors unless you have all rights, consent, and legal authority required in your location.

Your responsibilities

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the rights, permissions, and consent needed for any media, faces, likenesses, audio, or other materials you import, edit, export, or share. Do not use the app for deception, harassment, impersonation, fraud, illegal activity, or violations of another person's rights.

Your choices

Because we do not operate user accounts or store your media on our servers, most privacy choices are controlled on your device. You can remove local app data by deleting projects, clearing exported media you saved, changing iOS permissions, or deleting the app.

If you contact us by email, we will use your email address and message only to respond to your request and maintain necessary support records. We do not use support emails for advertising.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves, including if we add new features, new data practices, or new support channels. The "Last updated" date above identifies the latest version.
